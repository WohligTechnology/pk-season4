KNOW  About Us

Finance is a major part of the world and it is interlinked with almost every aspect of our lives and can be called as the lifeblood of the modern world. Knowingly or unknowingly, we all make major and minor financial decisions daily. Pehla Kadam is an initiative by CNBC AWAAZ that aims to impart knowledge that is crucial to make well-informed and ultimately intelligent financial decisions. Did you know that CNBC AWAAZ is the leading Hindi Business News Channel in India? In fact, it has managed to create quite an impact on consumers and administrators alike.

Pehla Kadam is a strategically designed platform that provides ideas and creates products for people who are looking for a better way to manage their finances. In fact, Pehla Kadam has something to offer for everyone - from corporates to first-time investors. Everything in this world is related to finance and therefore it is important to learn about it. Knowledge is the best weapon to have in your arsenal, if you want to become a well-informed investor. Pehla Kadam is the one-stop guide for all those who want to improve their knowledge about the financial world.
