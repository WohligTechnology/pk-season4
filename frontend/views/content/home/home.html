KNOW  About Us

Finance is a major part of the world and it is interlinked with almost every aspect of our lives and can be called as the lifeblood of the modern world. Knowingly or unknowingly, we all make major and minor financial decisions daily. Pehla Kadam is an initiative by CNBC AWAAZ that aims to impart knowledge that is crucial to make well-informed and ultimately intelligent financial decisions. Did you know that CNBC AWAAZ is the leading Hindi Business News Channel in India? In fact, it has managed to create quite an impact on consumers and administrators alike.

Pehla Kadam boasts of wide reach across rural and urban India. The “Rural Financial Literacy Program” is one of the noteworthy programs of Pehla Kadam. This program helps to further the goal to impart knowledge to the rural sectors of the country for strategically managing their finances.
